About Marie

She built it
from her kitchen.

Marie Assi is a Dubai-based food creator, body confidence advocate, and community builder who turned her love of cooking and her unapologetic way of living into a movement that 150,000+ women follow every single day.

She's not just a content creator. She's a teacher who believes that cooking is confidence — that knowing how to feed yourself with flavour and love is one of the most radical acts of self-care. Her recipes are bold, her methods are real, and her community is unlike anything else online.

Now she's packaging everything she knows — every recipe, every lesson, every hard-won insight from years in the kitchen — into programmes, courses, and a membership community for women who want to live homegrown.
